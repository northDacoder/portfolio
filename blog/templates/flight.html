the experience

Flight is a gathering built by developers, for developers. You’ll feel right at home if you live, breathe and occasionally dream in Java, Objective-C, or Swift. Following the morning keynote, you’ll dive into a series of technical sessions and cap off the day with a great happy hour where you can meet and mingle with Twitter’s engineering team.

Meet & Mingle

Product Pods and Lightning Theatre

Beginning at lunch and all afternoon, visit our Product Pods to meet our engineers. Drop by the Lightning Theatre to hear shorter talks about Twitter data, Twitter’s open source initiatives, and scalability tips from Twitter’s engineers.

Happy Hour

Celebrate the day and mingle with the Twitter team and your peers over food, drinks, games and music. Please note: You must be 21+ and wearing our credentials to drink alcohol at this party.

getting there

Public Transportation

Bill Graham Civic Auditorium is accessible via BART and Muni Transit (Civic Center station). The San Francisco Caltrain station is a 7-minute ride away.

Bike Parking

Secure bike parking will be available at Bill Graham Civic Auditorium for all attendees.

Parking

The Civic Center Plaza Garage is located at 355 McAllister Street. It’s open from 6am-12am. A full day rate is $27.00.
